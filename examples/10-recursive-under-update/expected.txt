Example 10: Recursive Under Update (Transitive Closure)
=======================================================

=== step 1: insert edges a->b->c->d ===
+ reach("a", "b")
+ reach("a", "c")
+ reach("a", "d")
+ reach("b", "c")
+ reach("b", "d")
+ reach("c", "d")

=== step 2: remove edge(b,c) ===
- reach("a", "c")
- reach("a", "d")
- reach("b", "c")
- reach("b", "d")

=== step 3: re-insert edge(b,c) ===
+ reach("a", "c")
+ reach("a", "d")
+ reach("b", "c")
+ reach("b", "d")

Done.
