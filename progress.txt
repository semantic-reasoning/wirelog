### Performance Findings

1. **Memory amplification IMPROVED**: CSPA peak RSS dropped from 4.67GB to 2.27GB (-52%)
   - Incremental consolidate eliminates old_data snapshots per iteration
   - Delta computation done via merge walk, not full sorting

2. **Iteration count likely UNCHANGED**: The 20% slowdown on CSPA suggests:
   - Option 1 may not be achieving projected O(D log D + N) speedup
   - Or baseline iteration count still has H2 incompleteness

3. **CSDA regression unexpected**: 7.1ms → 13.3ms suggests something in the core evaluation path is slower
